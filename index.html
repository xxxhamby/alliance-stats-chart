Features

  • Weighted "True Score" ranking (Lethality x1.2, Health x1.1).
  • Automatic stat growth/decline tracking with red/green indicators.
  • Detailed, expandable player history logs with revert functionality.
  • Single-player vs. Alliance Average comparison charts (double-click row).
  • Multi-player comparison charts (up to 5 players).
  • Troop Specialization identifiers (Infantry, Cavalry, Archer, Balanced).
  • Comprehensive Alliance Analytics dashboard.
  • Multi-language support with persistent selection.
